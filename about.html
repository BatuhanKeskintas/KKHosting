Our Story

Where Hospitality Meets Home

KK Hosting was born from a simple but powerful idea: that premium hospitality doesn't have to mean impersonal hotels, and that staying in an apartment doesn't have to mean compromising on quality.

Based in Warsaw, Poland, we started with a single apartment and a commitment to doing things right. We focused on excellence — from the quality of our linens to the responsiveness of our guest support. Word spread, and so did our portfolio.

Today, KK Hosting manages a curated collection of premium apartments across Warsaw's most desirable neighborhoods. Each property is handpicked, professionally styled, and maintained to standards that rival the finest boutique hotels.

But what truly sets us apart is our approach: we believe every guest deserves a seamless, comfortable, and genuinely warm experience. That's not a tagline — it's how we operate, every single day.
